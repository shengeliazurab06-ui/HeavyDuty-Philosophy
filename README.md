# 🏋️ Mike Mentzer – Heavy Duty Philosophy

This is a tribute website dedicated to Mike Mentzer and his famous "Heavy Duty" training system. The site explains his life, his logic-based philosophy, and his legacy in bodybuilding.

---

## ✨ What is this site about?
- **Biography:** Learn about Mike Mentzer's life.
- **Philosophy:** Simple explanation of "Heavy Duty" (Logic and Intensity).
- **Training & Nutrition:** How he trained and what he ate.
- **Legacy:** Information about Dorian Yates, Ray Mentzer, and the future of High Intensity Training.

## 🛠️ Built With
- **HTML5:** For the structure and content.
- **CSS3:** For the dark, professional design and responsive layout (works on mobile too!).
- **Font Awesome:** For the icons.

## 🚀 How to Open
To view the website:
1. Make sure the `index.html` and `style.css` files are in the same folder.
2. Open **`index.html`** in your web browser.
